Ship's Log

A diary by Capt. Yucheng 'Curly' Zhanglitty

Nautilus has been lurking the depths of the Black Sea for centuries. It is rumored to be over 900 years old.

Leviathan is the largest documented Kraken. Though we've never seen it fully emerged from the water, we estimate it to be over 200 metres long.

Colosus is known for its bright red coloration. Survivors of ships attacked by this Kraken say its piercing blue eyes are the first thing you see glowing in the murky water.
